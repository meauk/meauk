Component
It is one part of one Numeral Accomposix. There are six kinds of sets as its variation:
  1. Ones (split into two types):
    1. Lastly-Located Ones
    2. Penultimately-Located Ones
  2. Tens
  3. Hundreds
  4. Thousands
  5. Ten-Thousands
  6. Hundred-Thousands
Numeral Accomposix
It is one part of one Numeral Substem. There are five categories as its variation, and each consists of one-to-six kinds of componential sets from the identical category. Those from the same category cannot be put continually.
  1. Cardinal
    • the only Latin-origin part in one illion number

  2. Ordinal

      (It can be put immediately after two kinds of Precomposixes only.)
    • the Fundamental Tier-Elucidator
      (It can elucidate the tier located between the first and the 999,999th.)
  3. Adverbial

      (It is always put immediately before the Subcomposix.)
