Segment
is each part constituting an affix.
Element
is each part constituting an segment.
Stem
is a free-morpheme element in a segment.
Component
is a bound-morpheme element, which is connected to a stem.
  • Initiator: Prefix-Equivalent; before a stem
  • Mediator: Interfix-Equivalent; between two stems
  • Intercalator: Infix-Equivalent; inside a stem.
  • Terminator: Suffix-Equivalent; after a stem
  • Encapsulator: Circumfix-Equivalent; around a Stem
  • Interpolator: Transfix-Equivalent; through a Stem
  • Alternator: Simulfix-Equivalent; with a segment of a stem being changed
  • Modulator: Suprafix-Equivalent; with a suprasegmental feature of a stem being changed
  • Eliminator: Disfix-Equivalent; with a portion of a stem being elided
  • Repduplicator: Duplifix-Equivalent; with a reduplicated portiom of a stem being incorporated
